Our Mission

Celebrate. Enhance. Support. Save.

After prayer and guidance from the Holy Spirit, Keep The Covenant was founded to celebrate, enhance, support, and save marriages. We believe that when marriages are strengthened, families are strengthened — and communities are transformed.

Originating from Turning Point Bible Fellowship Church in Austin, Texas, we now serve couples as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, partnering with TPBFC and LifeChangers Inc. in this vital ministry.

Local Marriage & Counseling Resources

Trusted Christian counseling centers and marriage support services in the greater Austin area. These are organizations we recommend for couples seeking professional guidance.

Christian Counseling Austin

Licensed Marriage Counseling

Specializes in Christian marriage counseling for couples considering divorce, communication issues, and infidelity recovery. Licensed Professional Counselor with a Master of Arts in Counseling and Master of Divinity.

5000 Bee Caves Rd, Ste 100, Austin TX 78746
In-person & online sessions available throughout Texas

Visit Website →

Abundant Life Counseling Services

Faith-Based Counseling Center

Christian counseling center serving the greater Austin area from three locations. Offers marriage and premarital counseling, family conflict counseling, and individual therapy from a Christian perspective.

Locations in North Austin, Kyle, and Georgetown
Multiple price points available

Visit Website →

Austin Stone Counseling

Gospel-Centered Counseling

Provides gospel-centered counseling focused on building strong foundations for dating, engaged, and married couples. Offers intentional communication support and relational healing through a faith-based approach.

Multiple Austin locations
Out-of-network insurance receipts provided

Visit Website →

Neema Counseling

Christian Marriage & Family Therapy

Offers Christian marriage counseling combining biblical insight with professional therapy. Whether your marriage is in crisis or needs a tune-up, they provide tools for communication, forgiveness, and intimacy.

Austin & Houston locations · Virtual available
Phone: (512) 669-5701

Visit Website →

Decades Counseling

Premarital & Couples Therapy

Uses the Prepare/Enrich program across 6–8 sessions to help couples understand relationship strengths and growth areas. All counselors are certified in Prepare/Enrich with a faith-centered approach.

Downtown Austin · In-person & virtual
Works with Austin-area churches

Visit Website →

Affair Recovery

Infidelity & Crisis Recovery

Provides help and resources for healing after infidelity. Their mission is to restore those in crisis to extraordinary lives of meaning and purpose, combining a faith-based curriculum with support groups.

Austin-based · Online programs available
For couples & individuals

Visit Website →

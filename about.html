About Our Ministry

Our Story

Founded in Faith, Driven by Purpose

After prayer and guidance from the Holy Spirit, Dr. Christopher and Shaneen Harvey founded Keep The Covenant in 2014 with a clear mission: to celebrate, enhance, support, and save marriages. What began as a calling from God has grown into a thriving ministry serving couples across the Austin area and beyond.

Rooted in Turning Point Bible Fellowship Church under the pastoral leadership of Carl & Linda Harvey Sr., the ministry now operates as a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization in partnership with TPBFC and LifeChangers Inc. We are a Bible-based ministry, and we believe marriage was created by God for the union of man and woman — with the primary purpose of bringing God glory.

Today, our leadership team has grown to include three dedicated couples who share a passion for strengthening marriages and building up godly families in our community.

Founders & Lead Facilitators

Dr. Christopher & Shaneen Harvey

Chris and Shaneen are the heart behind Keep The Covenant. Since founding the ministry in 2014, they have faithfully led monthly enrichment classes, facilitated couple mentoring, and organized romantic giveaways — all driven by a desire to see marriages thrive the way God intended.

Mentored by Pastor Carl & Linda Harvey Sr., who bring over 30 years of Christian marriage counseling experience, the Harveys regularly attend conferences and seminars worldwide to sharpen their facilitation and strengthen their own marriage.

✦Dr. Christopher Harvey — Doctoral degree in Educational Leadership; 19+ years working with families in educational and community settings
✦Shaneen Harvey — Honors degree in Psychology; 13+ years mentoring women, including women in prison and victims of fraud
✦Certified couple mentors through Love and Respect & Dare to Be Different programs

Premarital Counseling

Preparing for marriage is one of the most exciting seasons of your life — and one of the most important times to invest in your relationship. We strongly recommend premarital counseling for every engaged couple. Many churches in the Austin area provide it for free or at a nominal cost.

In Texas, couples who complete a valid premarital counseling program may qualify for a discounted marriage license fee and a waiver of the 72-hour waiting period. It's a practical benefit on top of the relational growth you'll experience.

Post-Marital Counseling

Already married and need a tune-up? Post-marital counseling helps couples evaluate where they are versus where they expected to be. It's not a sign of failure — it's a sign of wisdom. These sessions help you recalibrate, address unresolved issues, and get back on the same page.

How We Help

  • We connect you with trusted Christian counseling providers in Austin
  • Referrals are personalized based on your specific needs and situation
  • We work with churches, licensed counselors, and faith-based organizations
  • This is a referral service — we help you find the right fit, not provide counseling directly

Our Services

Everything we offer to help your marriage flourish

Free · Virtual

Monthly Enrichment Classes

Our signature offering — free virtual classes held 8–10 times per year. Each session covers a key marriage topic with practical skills you can apply immediately. Expect lively discussions, couple activities, and a welcoming community of couples investing in their relationship.

  • Fun activities, games, and icebreakers
  • Biblically grounded lessons on real marriage topics
  • Small group discussions with other couples
  • Raffles, romantic giveaways, and community
Learn More
6–10 Weeks · One-on-One

Couple Mentoring

Marriage mentoring is for couples who want to strengthen their relationship — whether your marriage is strong or struggling. We've partnered with Love and Respect and Dare to Be Different to offer individual mentoring led by trained mentor couples.

  • Meet with a trained mentor couple weekly for 6–10 weeks
  • Each session is approximately 1.5 hours
  • Biblically based approach developed by founder Matt Loehr
  • In-depth survey to identify areas needing strengthening

Date Night Challenges

Referral Service

Pre & Post-Marital Counseling

Preparing for marriage? We strongly recommend premarital counseling. Many churches provide it for free or at a nominal cost, and some Texas counties even discount marriage license fees upon completion of a valid counseling program.

Already married and need a tune-up? We also connect couples with post-marital counseling services that compare previous expectations with current needs.

Learn More
Professional · Licensed

Licensed Counseling

Sometimes unresolved issues surface and create challenges in marriage. Poor communication, financial stress, and intimacy struggles are among the leading causes of divorce. When your marriage needs professional support, we connect you with trusted Christian licensed professional counselors in the Austin area.

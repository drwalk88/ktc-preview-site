Join the Team

Use your gifts to strengthen marriages and serve couples in our community

Serve With Us

Be Part of Something Bigger

Keep The Covenant exists because faithful people choose to give their time, talents, and hearts to this ministry. Whether you're looking to serve behind the scenes, lead alongside our team, or use a specific gift God has given you — there's a place for you here.

We're a growing ministry with a growing need for passionate couples and individuals who believe in the power of biblical marriage. If you feel called to invest in marriages and families, we'd love to hear from you.

Opportunities

Ways to Get Involved

🤝

Mentor Couple

Walk alongside a couple through our 6–10 week biblically based mentoring program. Training is provided through our partnership with Love and Respect and Dare to Be Different.

🎓

Class Facilitator

Help lead or co-facilitate our monthly virtual enrichment classes. You'll guide discussions, lead activities, and create a welcoming environment for couples to learn and grow.

📱

Social Media & Content

Help us create and share marriage encouragement, event promotions, and ministry highlights across our social media platforms. Photography and design skills are a plus.

🎉

Events & Outings

Help plan, coordinate, and execute our couples outings, date night events, and special gatherings. Great for people who love creating memorable experiences.

🙏

Prayer Team

Commit to praying regularly for our ministry, our couples, and the marriages in our community. Receive prayer requests and updates to guide your intercession.

💡

General Volunteer

Not sure where you fit? That's okay. Tell us about your gifts and interests, and we'll find a way to plug you in where you'll make the greatest impact.

What We Look For

Heart Over Resume

You don't need a degree in counseling or years of ministry experience. What matters most is a heart for God and a desire to see marriages thrive.

✦

A Heart for Marriage

You believe in the power of biblical marriage and want to help others experience it.

✦

A Growing Faith

You are actively pursuing your own walk with Christ and growing in your relationship with God.

✦

Reliability & Commitment

You show up when you say you will and follow through on what you've committed to.

✦

A Teachable Spirit

You're open to learning, growing, and receiving feedback as part of a team.

✦

Respect for Confidentiality

Ministry involves sensitive topics. Discretion and trust are essential.

✦

A Servant's Heart

You're motivated by love and a desire to serve — not recognition or platform.
